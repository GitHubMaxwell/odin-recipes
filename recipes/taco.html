Taco Salad

Description

An easy an fast but tasty dinner. Great for a Teusday dinner.

Often served with boxed spanish rice on the side

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • taco seasoning
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • shredded cheese
  • sour cream
  • tortilla chips

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef and chop it in the pan to be small crumbles.
  2. If there is a lot of excess fat in the pan, spoon some out.
  3. Add the taco seasoning mix and water (as directed on the taco seasoning mix packet)
  4. Wash and shred lettuce
  5. Dice tomatoes
  6. Assemble the taco bowl by first adding lettuce, then meat, then cheese, then tomato, then sour cream (optional), and lastly crumble some tortilla chips over top.
